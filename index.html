About Me

My name is Ebuka, and I'm a computer science student. Ever since I was young, I’ve been fascinated by how technology works and how it shapes the world around us. I remember spending hours tinkering with my family’s old computer, trying to understand how software and hardware interact. That curiosity eventually led me to pursue computer science as my major. I’m currently deep into coding, algorithms, and data structures, and while it can be challenging, I find immense satisfaction in solving complex problems. Whether it’s building a simple website or developing a more intricate application, every project feels like a new adventure. There are moments when I get stuck, of course, but the sense of accomplishment when I finally crack a problem is unbeatable. In the future, I hope to work in software development or artificial intelligence, but for now, I’m focused on building a strong foundation in computer science. The journey is just beginning, and I’m excited to see where it takes me.
